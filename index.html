ABOUT ME

Hey! I'm Tapzi — an avgeek and a livery painter for flight simulators based in Israel.

I specialize in realistic and high-quality repaints from all over the world, ranging from less known airlines to the most known ones, and also fully custom liveries for individuals and virtual airlines.

Whether it's a full fleet pack or a custom livery, I aim to make each one unique and detailed as much as possible. I'm also part of teams like HUES, LivToAir, and FSLTL.
