UPCOMING LIVERY PROJECTS

AirAsia

Mega pack project of AirAsia liveries for the Fenix Airbus A320Ceo. It includes registrations from the whole AirAsia group (Indonesia, Malaysia, Thailand, Japan, Philippines, and India). In addition, the Mega Pack will include some of AirAsia's special liveries as well as cabin textures for all liveries.
If you have any references or info that you think might help with this project, please contact me!

Regional Airlines

A collection of less-known/defunct airline liveries mainly (but not only) for the Fenix Airbuses. The pack will include airlines such as Aigle Azur, Strategic Airlines, BestAir, Air Europe, and many more.

Stay tuned for the livery releases!
